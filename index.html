Empowering Students with skills to make a difference

At Slicky Robotics, our mission is to provide students with access to the highly demanded skills of Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence and the Internet Of Things

Our ultimate goal is to provide an education that will empower students to innovate and have confidence in the impact they can make, regardless of their previous technolgical experience.

The Challenge

We can't apply most of the material we learn in school. It’s time to change that.

Internships and jobs require previous practical experience to get practical experience.
Students fear and shy away from STEM fields because they don't know the impact that they can have.
There are high barriers to entry to learn about AI, ML, or IOT. Most instruction is only offered at top-tier schools.

Our Solution

We create simple kits, where students can follow instructions to build entertaining projects that will teach them invaluable skills

Slikcy, the robot who gets low.

Given a microcontroller, 3-D printed pieces, and electrical wires, students can build Slicky, Slicky Robotics' first product, from scratch. Along their journey they can learn about circuits, simple programming, the internet of things, robotics, and some sick dance moves to show at their next party.
